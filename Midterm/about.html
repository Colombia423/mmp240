About Stranger Things

"Stranger Things" is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The show follows a group of kids who encounter mysterious government experiments, a parallel dimension called the Upside Down, and supernatural beings like the Demogorgon.

Main Cast

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair
