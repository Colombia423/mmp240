About the Show

Breaking Bad is an American TV series created by Vince Gilligan. It follows the life of Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher who turns to making methamphetamine to provide for his family after being diagnosed with terminal cancer. This intense, character-driven drama explores the consequences of his actions and the transformation of his persona from a mild-mannered teacher to a ruthless drug kingpin.

Main Cast

  • Bryan Cranston as Walter White
  • Aaron Paul as Jesse Pinkman
  • Anna Gunn as Skyler White
  • Dean Norris as Hank Schrader
  • RJ Mitte as Walter White Jr.
