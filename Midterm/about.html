About the Show

"Breaking Bad" is a critically acclaimed American TV drama series created by Vince Gilligan. The series follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer, and his descent into the criminal underworld. Set in Albuquerque, New Mexico, it explores themes of morality, family, and the consequences of one's actions.

Main Cast

  • Bryan Cranston as Walter White
  • Aaron Paul as Jesse Pinkman
  • Anna Gunn as Skyler White
  • Dean Norris as Hank Schrader
  • RJ Mitte as Walter White Jr.
