Chapter One: The Vanishing of Will Byers

In the first episode of Stranger Things, a young boy named Will Byers goes missing. His friends and family begin to search for him, uncovering strange occurrences that lead them to discover the terrifying Upside Down world.

Chapter Two: The Weirdo on Maple Street

After discovering Eleven, the kids start to realize she has extraordinary abilities. Meanwhile, Will's mother, Joyce, believes he is trying to communicate with her from another world.
