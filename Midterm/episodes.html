Episodes

Season 1, Episode 1: "Pilot"

Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with terminal cancer, teams up with former student Jesse Pinkman to cook and sell methamphetamine to secure his family's future.

Season 1, Episode 2: "Cat's in the Bag..."

After a disastrous first cook, Walter and Jesse try to clean up the mess. They deal with the aftermath of their actions and start to question their ability to be criminals.

Season 1, Episode 3: "And the Bag's in the River"

Walter struggles with his conscience as the reality of his new life as a meth manufacturer sets in. Jesse faces the consequences of his actions.

Season 1, Episode 4: "Cancer Man"

Walter's cancer diagnosis forces him to confront his past while continuing to maintain his secret life. The pressure of his double life increases.

Season 1, Episode 5: "Gray Matter"

Walter's financial situation becomes more strained, and he faces a dilemma about accepting help from his old friends, Gretchen and Elliott.

Season 1, Episode 6: "Crazy Handful of Nothin'"

Walter confronts a dangerous drug dealer and proves he's willing to do whatever it takes to ensure his family's safety.

Season 1, Episode 7: "A No-Rough-Stuff-Type Deal"

As Walter's actions become more violent and his life more complicated, he must make a choice between his family and his newfound life in the drug trade.
